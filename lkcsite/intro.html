COMPUTER DEPARTMENT

The Department of Computer Science & IT was set up under the aegis of Lyallpur Khalsa College, Jalandhar a premier institution of Northern India, imparting quality education to more than 6000 students including more than 2600 girls. The department is funded by DBT under Star College Scheme. Today, it is popularly acknowledged as a center of excellence in the region. It provides professionals to the industry with skills honed to assume exalted leadership positions at national and international IT spectrum. The Department has set high academic standards and excelled in thought leadership by providing a pool of creative genius endowed with technical competence.
